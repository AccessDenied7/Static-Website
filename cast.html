Legacy

This musical has an extremely diverse casting. Actors of color play the Founding Fathers and historical figures, reframing American history for a modern audience. Hamilton revolutionised Broadway musicals by fusing the past and present in a way that made history seem relevant and alive. Around the world, its influence on pop culture, theatre, and education is evident.
