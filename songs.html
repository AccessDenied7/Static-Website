The music

🎶

The score for Hamilton is unlike anything that Broadway has ever heard. There is hip-hop & rap for wit, quickness and character conflicts. In addition, we get classic Broadway show tunes that pay homage to Broadway heritage, as well as R&B and soul for emotional depth (such as "Wait For It" or "Satisfied"). Wordplay, recurrent themes, and historical references appear throughout the songs. Some songs, like as "My Shot," echo throughout the show as themes of mortality and ambition. This score is loved worldwide, with song's like "The room where it happens" and "Helpless" being fan favourites.

Achievements

11 Tony Awards, including Best Musical, were won by Hamilton. Additionally, the show was awarded the 2016 Pulitzer Prize for Drama. The Grammy for Best Musical Theatre Album went to it. With global tours and a Disney+ film adaptation, the musical achieved unprecedented success.





Fun fact!

The original cast of Hamilton returned for a stunning 10-year reunion at the 78th Annual Tony Awards on June 8, 2025, ending a ten-year break and receiving an overwhelming standing ovation. They opened with the fiery Lin-Manuel Miranda and Leslie Odom Jr. song "Non-Stop," then sped through hits like "My Shot" and "The Schuyler Sisters," before ending with "History Has Its Eyes on You" and "Alexander Hamilton."
